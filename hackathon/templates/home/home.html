What our model does

Our Machine Learning Based model is used to find similarities in two different pictures and alert the user of any potential matches

What is its use

We use this model for an AI based Lost and Found website for helping students who have lost their valuables.

Login/Sign-up = To get notified about your missing/found item login by clicking the Login button on the top. New User? Sign up!

Lost Something? = Click the lost button on the top and fill the form. The AI will show you the most similar image from our database. If it is yours we will notify the finder via E-Mail. In case it is not yours, we will notify you via E-Mail if a suitable match is found.

Found Something? = Click the found button on the top and fill the form. The AI will show you the most matching image from our database. If it matches we will notify the claimant via an E-Mail. In case it is not what you uploaded, we will notify you via E-Mail if a claimant is found.
